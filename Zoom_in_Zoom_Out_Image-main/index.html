Why do we use it?

Businesses use images and graphics extensively in advertising and marketing materials to attract and engage potential customers. Visuals can help create an emotional connection with a brand or product!

Where can I get some?

If you're looking for specific photos, you can ask for permission from individuals on social media or other online platforms to use their images. Always obtain proper consent and provide credit when necessary. Remember that when using images, it's important to consider copyright and licensing restrictions!

Where does it come from?

Websites like Unsplash, Pexels, and Pixabay provide high-quality photos that you can use for free, even for commercial purposes. You can use search engines like Google Images to find pictures, but make sure to filter your search for images that are labeled for reuse or are in the public domain if you plan to use them for any public or commercial purpose.
